Animals of Instagram

In today's internet world goats, foxes and even opossums are ruling Instagram and spreading awareness of the different species through a bigger platform.

Juniper the Friendly Fox

Juniper was adopted and lives her life as a real life Fox and the Hound with her dog friend Moose. Juniper has her own website where her owner gives helpful information and her own Fox Shop where she sells paintings, calendars and apparel.
